## Tips for getting participation

1. **Lead with purpose, not technology** — "preserve our language" motivates more
   than "machine learning dataset."
2. **Make the first ask tiny** — "add one sentence" beats "contribute to a corpus."
3. **Show progress publicly** — "We reached 9,000 sentences this week!" keeps momentum.
4. **Credit contributors** — list names (with permission) in the project README.
5. **Seed it yourself first** — add 20–30 pairs before inviting others, so the Sheet
   doesn't look empty.
